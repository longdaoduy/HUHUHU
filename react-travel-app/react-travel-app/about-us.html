Mission

We aim to create a useful tool to help tourists and people of Vietnam discover, record and share the beauty of the country's unique landmarks. Through AI and machine learning technology, we hope to bring a smart and personalized travel experience.

Vision

Become the leading smart travel platform in Vietnam, connecting millions of people who love to explore. We hope to contribute to promoting the culture, history and beautiful places of Vietnam to international friends, while promoting sustainable and responsible tourism.
